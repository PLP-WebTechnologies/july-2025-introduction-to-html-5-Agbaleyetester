Geography of Nigeria

Nigeria is a country in West Africa located at Latitude 9.08'N and Latitude 8.67'E, these coordinates place Nigeria in both the Northern and Eastern hemispheres, bording the Gulf of Guinea in the South, Benin in the West, Chad and Cameroon in the East, and Niger in the North.

Nigeria has a tropical climate, with hight temperatures and high humidty throughout the year. The country experiences two main seasoms: a rainy seasons(April - October) and a dry season (November - March).

Nigeria and Its Geopolitical Zones

Nigeria is divided into six geopolitical zones, each with distinct characteristics and states.

  1. North Central Zone
